Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from Trello or Notion?
FLOW works completely offline, costs $29 once (not $10+/month), and keeps your data private on your machine. Trello and Notion require internet, subscriptions, and store your data in their cloud.
Can I collaborate with my team?
FLOW is designed for personal use. For team collaboration, you can export boards and share the files, or use a shared folder with Dropbox/iCloud. Real-time collaboration requires the cloud ‚Äî which we're avoiding for privacy.
Does it sync between my laptop and desktop?
Not built-in, but you can use any sync service you trust. Put the FLOW folder in Dropbox, iCloud, or Syncthing. Your data is standard JSON files that sync perfectly.
What happens to my data if I stop using it?
You own your data completely. It's stored as JSON files that any developer can read. Export anytime, migrate anywhere. No lock-in, ever.
Is there a mobile app?
Not yet. FLOW works in any modern browser, so you can use it on mobile browsers. A dedicated mobile app is on our roadmap.

30-day money-back guarantee, no questions asked. If FLOW doesn't work for you, we'll refund every penny.
